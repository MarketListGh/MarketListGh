About MarketList

At MarketList, we understand that shopping can be time-consuming, stressful, and sometimes inconvenient—especially for busy Ghanaians. That’s why we created MarketList: a trusted service that brings your favorite local markets straight to your doorstep.

Whether it’s fresh groceries from Makola Market, daily essentials from Accra Mall, or home and electrical appliances, MarketList connects you with reliable local shoppers who deliver your items safely and promptly.

Our Mission

To make shopping effortless for every Ghanaian by providing seamless access to popular local markets, ensuring convenience, reliability, and satisfaction with every order.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted local shopping assistant across Ghana, empowering communities with easy access to quality products.

Why Choose MarketList?

  • Convenience: Skip long lines and traffic; orders delivered to your home.
  • Trusted Shoppers: Vetted shoppers handle your orders carefully.
  • Wide Range of Products: From groceries to appliances, get what you need.
  • Transparent Pricing: Know your budget, service fees, and total.
  • Customer Support: Assistance with questions, orders, and smooth experience.

Our Values

  • Customer-Centric: Your satisfaction comes first.
  • Reliability: Accurate orders and timely delivery every time.
  • Trust: Only trusted shoppers handle orders.
  • Innovation: Continuously improving for faster, easier experience.
