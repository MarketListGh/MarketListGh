About MarketList

At MarketList, we understand that shopping can be time-consuming, stressful, and sometimes inconvenient—especially for busy Ghanaians with work, family, and daily commitments. That’s why we created MarketList: a trusted service that brings your favorite local markets straight to your doorstep.

Whether it’s fresh groceries from Makola Market, daily essentials from Accra Mall, or home and electrical appliances, MarketList connects you with reliable local shoppers who purchase your items and deliver them safely and promptly, saving you time and effort.

Our Mission

To make shopping effortless for every Ghanaian by providing seamless access to popular local markets, ensuring convenience, reliability, and satisfaction with every order.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and widely recognized local shopping assistant across Ghana, empowering communities with easy access to quality products.

Why Choose MarketList?

  • Convenience: Skip long lines and traffic; orders are delivered to your home.
  • Trusted Shoppers: We work with vetted local shoppers who handle your orders carefully.
  • Wide Range of Products: From groceries to appliances, we ensure you get what you need.
  • Transparent Pricing: Know exactly your budget, service fees, and total.
  • Customer Support: We assist with questions, orders, and smooth experience.

Our Values

  • Customer-Centric: Your convenience and satisfaction come first.
  • Reliability: Accurate orders and timely delivery every time.
  • Trust: Only trusted shoppers handle your orders with care.
  • Innovation: Continuously improving our platform for a faster, easier experience.

At MarketList, shopping is simple, enjoyable, and stress-free. Whether for weekly groceries, special items, or last-minute needs, MarketList is here to make your life easier—because your time matters.
