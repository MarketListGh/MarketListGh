About MarketList

MarketList helps customers in Ghana purchase groceries, clothing, home and electrical appliances from popular markets and delivers them to your doorstep. We connect you with local market shoppers who buy for you and ensure safe delivery.

Mission

To simplify market shopping and save time for Ghanaians by bringing market access to your door.

Vision

To be the most trusted local shopping assistant across Ghana.
